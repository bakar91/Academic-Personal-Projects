K-Means clustering analysis is performed on the chemical compostion of 40 Ceramics samples obtained from Longquan & Jingdezhen regions in China. The main objectives of the task are:
 - Categorizing samples obtained from Longquan & Jingdezhen based on thier chemical compostion
 - Clustering glazing techniques between different ages the samples were procured from i.e. Song Dynasty, Yuan Dynasty & Ming Dynasty based on their compostion
